Shattered Dynasty

One of my favourite games I have made, Shattered Dynasty, is a 2D platformer set in a post-apocoliptic Sogun era.

I created this game to get started with both 2D pixel art and to get used to Unity's UI and system mechanics..

Main Ideas

* I expanded my knowledge of Unity as a whole and tried creating my own platformer.

* Adding a grappling hook and more physics - based movement to set it apart from other platformers .

Knight Watch

Great ideas spark great games. This game got me started in the field of FPS games and the mechanics behind them .

The idea of creating an FPS game where you shoot knights is just silly enough that it might work , and I'm looking forward to improving this in the future .

It introduced me to animation in 3D and different ways of collision in a 3D game.

Main Ideas

* FPS in medavial times

* Guns and explosions make games fun

Asteroids Destroyer

While on my learning journey, I started thinking back on older games and how they would stand up in today's gaming industry .

This sparked the idea of creating a 3D asteroids- inspired game with entirely new movement and random asteroid generation.

Main Ideas

* Asteriods but in 3D

* Add more features like a boost
